About Me

Who am I?

Hi ! Im Ryan.

Im a 20 year old Fine Arts in Digital Arts University student. I am currently undertaking and completing a BFA degree in 2019, to further improve my skills and knowledge in the Graphic Design Industry.

Im a football fanatic, an adventurer, a gym addict, and a humourous lad and am super passionate about design and photography. I have always had a liking towards design, since a very young age!

Throughout my 3 year course at Uni, I have got to experience a variety of new and unusual techniques and mediums, which I have never experimented or ever thought of doing. Yet I am proud to say that through those hard new experiences I got to find new likings and new passions towards the emotion of 'feeling discomfort' through design and hopefully succeeding to produce works that are close to heart!

“There are three responses to a piece of design– yes, no, and WOW! Wow is the one to aim for.”

- Milton Glasier
